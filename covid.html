COVID-19 policies
Updated 01-03-22
In order to protect ourselves and the public, we have put in place some new policies. Please make sure you read and are familiar with the policies. Failure to follow policies will result in not being allowed in. If you have questions, please ask.
General COVID-19 policies
  • During this global pandemic, in order to keep our staff and clients as safe as we can, we are currently prioritizing appointments. Walk-ins are available when there is room available. Call ahead to check for availability.
  • Clients are asked to fill out our online waiver before arriving for your appointment. If you need assistance please contact us.
  • Masks are still mandatory in this store, please wear one.
  • We have limited space for other people available, so please keep friends and support teams to a minumum.
  • Aftercare pamphlets are available online.
Tattoo COVID-19 policy
  • We are avoiding in-person consultations if possible. Please contact your artist directly through their email or instagram.
Piercing COVID-19 policy
  • Our piercers are available for piercings, consultations, troubleshooting, and advice by appointment only Wednesday-Monday. Walk-ins may be available, but it is recommended to call ahead.
  • Oral and nasal piercings are onli availavle for those who are fully vaccinated. Proof of vactination is required.
