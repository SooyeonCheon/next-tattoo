Tattoo and piercing policy
Tattoo policies
Our age policy has recently changed, as per the requirements of our insurance providers. We tattoo at 18 with valid government-issued photo ID. 16 and 17 year olds can get tattooed, however a legal guardian must be present to sign. Valid ID is required for both the client and guardian.
About custom tattoo
All of our artists have experience with smaller script and symbol tattoos, and these can usually be booked without a consultation.We are a custom shop, meaning that our talented artists can take your ideas and turn them into your perfect tattoo. For larger and custom pieces, we recommend perusing our artists’ portfolios and booking a free consultation with the artist whose style best fits the design you have in mind.
Piercing policies
There is no legal age of consent for piercing in the province of British Columbia. According to shop policy, you must be at least 16 years of age to be pierced without parental consent and at least 18 years of age for genital piercings. We require proof of age for all piercings. We accept parental consent for most piercings in person, with ID
About children’s piercing
If you are bringing your child to get pierced, it is recommended that you give yourself plenty of time as sometimes little ones need a break between ears to muster up the courage for the second one. If your child has a favorite comfort toy or treat, it might be helpful to bring that along with you. We do not use piercing guns as they are unsanitary, prone to coming out crooked/uneven, cause unnecessary tissue damage, and the jewellery is not designed promote healing. We emphasize the importance of informed consent. We will not pierce anyone who is unable to communicate that they want this piercing and that they know it will hurt. If at any point your child changes their mind, we will respect their wishes. Piercings are a permanent cosmetic alteration to the body and no one but the owner of the body being pierced can consent to that. Because of our values we will, under no circumstances, pierce infants.
About trans specific piercing
One of our piercers, Eann, specializes in issues regarding transgender clients, including pre-, post-, and non-op genital and nipple piercings, as well issues regarding HRT. This also includes being able to discuss what your plans may be in regards to transition and how that may affect the healing or placement of various piercings. Please call us or stop by if you have any questions or concerns you would like to discuss.
