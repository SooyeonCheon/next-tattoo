Waiver
To prevent unnecessary touching of shared surfaces and objects, and to speed up the sign-in process, waivers for tattoos and piercings are available online. Once completed they are automatically submitted to the system. Please try to complete the waiver before your appointment time. If you need assistance, one of our staff will be able to help you.
